Robo Soccer



"Every one has a Goal to achieve"

The Main Focus of the Robosoccer Competition is the Game of Soccer. Design a manually Controlled robot, wired or wireless that can push the ball in the opposite team's goal post and prevent opposite team from pushing the ball in their goal post. Fair play with game plan and stratergy will surely lead you to achieve your Goal.
