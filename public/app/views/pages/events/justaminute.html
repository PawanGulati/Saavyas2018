JUST A MINUTE

Can you beat the clock?

Just -A- Minute (or JAM) is an all round fun event that is all about the control of the mind over the mouth . Can you make it through sixty seconds of non-stop talking without hesistation ,repetiton or deviation ? The object of the game is for panellists to talk for sixty seconds on a given topic.
