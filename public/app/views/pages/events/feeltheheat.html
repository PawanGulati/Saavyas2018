Feel the Heat-Debate

A Gladiatorial combat of the intellect
Description:
Time spent arguing is, oddly enough, almost never wasted. To win this debate, don’t raise your voice, just improve your argument. It is better to debate a question without settling it than to settle a question without debating it. If you think you can argue better then feel free to barge into our super-heated debate.
