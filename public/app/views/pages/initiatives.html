Paper Recycling

REDUCE, RECYCLE, REUSE

As a commodity, Paper is the largest percentage of material thrown away. This initiative has been brought upon for the sole purpose of recycling and reusing this commodity. It has been observed that each ton of recycled paper can save 17 trees,380 gallons of oil,3 cubic yards of landfill space, lots of water and energy.

For this initiative, we request all the students from the institute to provide with any wastepaper materials which could be recyclable. The commodity would be sorted and sent for recycling and the financial income which is obtained could be used for the betterment of the institute and the students.

So by this initiative we would like to commence this drive which could not only help the society economically but also decrease our dependence of natural resources and help build a better eco-friendly environment.
